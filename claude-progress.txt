## What worked (session 2 findings)

### Core insight confirmed
Only MOMENTUM OSCILLATORS (RSI family) add independent filtering beyond BB_lower:
- RSI measures up/down close velocity — independent from price-level metrics
- StochRSI measures RSI's own extreme — even faster signal
- RSI(7) catches flash crashes; RSI(20) catches sustained selloffs
- These fire at DIFFERENT bars → genuinely complementary

### Failed oscillator classes
- CCI, WilliamsR: RANGE-BASED (measure price vs recent range) — correlated with BB_lower → too many trades (~240) → negative
- MFI: VOLUME-BASED (high-volume selling = capitulation that continues) → negative
- All non-RSI oscillators tried in rounds 11-14

### FastRSIRev tuning lessons
- Optimal BB: 2.18σ (same as MeanRevADX) — tighter 2.5σ filters good entries
- Optimal SL: -0.06 (not -0.04) — mean-reversion needs room to breathe; tight stops = more losers
- Optimal exit: RSI(7)>55 (not >50) — earlier exit clips recovery
- RSI(20)>30 filter: reduces DD (4.80→4.36%) by excluding MeanRevADX-territory entries

### StochRev tuning lessons
- Optimal stoch_k: <0.15 (not <0.10 — stricter filters good entries)
- Optimal ADX: >15 (not >19 — ADX>19 loses good trades for stoch-based entry)
- OR exit is critical (not AND)
